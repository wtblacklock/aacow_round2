Our Legacy

Trusted in
Central Texas
for Over 45 Years.

For over 45 years, Allergy & Asthma Care of Waco has served as the trusted choice for specialized allergy and asthma treatment in Waco, TX, and the greater Central Texas area.

We combine advanced medical expertise with a deep understanding of the local environmental factors unique to Central Texas — equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools to build personalized treatment plans.

We believe in more than just treating symptoms. Through one-on-one consultations and patient education, we give you the knowledge and resources to manage your health proactively — so you can rediscover the joy of a sneeze-free, wheeze-free life and embrace the beauty of the outdoors without limitations.

What We Stand For

Care That Goes
Beyond the Symptom.

Local Expertise,
Not Corporate Care

We aren't a franchise. We're a locally-owned practice that has served Central Texas families since 1978. Our doctors understand the specific allergens that thrive in Waco's climate — and they live here too.

Evidence-Based Medicine

Every treatment recommendation is grounded in current clinical research. Our physicians hold board certifications in Allergy & Immunology and participate in ongoing medical education to bring you the latest in care.

Whole-Family Care

From children with food allergies to adults battling decades of seasonal symptoms, we care for patients at every stage. One practice for the whole family — familiar faces, consistent care.
