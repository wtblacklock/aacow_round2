Have Questions?

We've got answers.

Short reads on what to expect — from how testing works to managing allergies in Central Texas.

  • Allergies are the immune system's exaggerated response to typically harmless substances like pollen, dust, or certain foods. Symptoms can include sneezing, itching, hives, swelling, and difficulty breathing. At Allergy & Asthma Care of Waco, we specialize in diagnosing and managing allergies for our patients in Texas.
  • At Allergy & Asthma Care of Waco, we specialize in treating a wide range of allergies and related conditions, including seasonal allergies, food allergies, asthma, eczema, and sinusitis.
  • While allergies cannot be cured, they can be effectively managed with proper treatment and lifestyle modifications. Our team is dedicated to helping you achieve long-term relief from your allergy symptoms, allowing you to live a healthier and happier life in Waco, Texas, and the greater Central Texas area.
  • We offer comprehensive allergy testing, including skin and blood tests, to accurately identify your allergy triggers. This helps us develop personalized treatment plans for optimal relief.
  • Immunotherapy, including allergy shots and drops, is a specialized treatment that builds immunity to allergens over time. This can significantly reduce allergy symptoms and improve your quality of life, especially for common allergens like pollen, dust, and pet dander.
  • Refilling your prescriptions is simple. You can request a refill by calling our office during business hours or by using our online patient portal. We will promptly process your request and notify you when your prescription is ready for pickup.
  • Yes — we offer advanced medications and targeted infusions to provide lasting relief for severe allergy and asthma symptoms. Our experienced healthcare providers will work with you to determine the most effective treatment options for your specific needs.
  • Managing food allergies requires specialized guidance to ensure safe diets and effective symptom management. Our team provides personalized support and resources to help you navigate potential life-threatening food allergies with confidence.
  • In the Waco area, common allergens such as pollen from oak, cedar, and ragweed, as well as mold spores, can exacerbate allergy symptoms. Our allergy testing can help identify your specific triggers, allowing us to tailor your treatment plan accordingly.
  • During allergy season in Waco, it's essential to take proactive measures to manage your symptoms. Our team can provide personalized recommendations, including medication management, environmental control strategies, and immunotherapy options, to help you stay comfortable and symptom-free.
  • Scheduling an appointment with us is easy. Simply give us a call at (254) 753-3646 or fill out our online appointment request form. Our friendly staff will assist you in finding a convenient time for your visit.
  • Yes, we offer virtual allergy care services. Telehealth consultations, online tracking, and customized treatment plans are available to provide accessible top-tier allergy and asthma care from the comfort of your own home.
